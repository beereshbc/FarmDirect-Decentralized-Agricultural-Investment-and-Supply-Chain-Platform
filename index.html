What is FarmDirect?

FarmDirect is a decentralized, AI-powered agricultural platform that enables individuals to invest directly in farming while managing the complete agricultural supply chain from cultivation to final sale.

We bridge the gap between traditional farming and modern investment, using technology to ensure transparency, reduce intermediaries, and empower local agricultural communities.
